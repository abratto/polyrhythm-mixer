Welcome to Alan's Polyrhythm Mixer
This mixer works at two rhythmic scales: wheel lanes divide one master cycle, while phrase sequencers run at each meter’s own pulse and unfold across multiple master cycles.
1. Pick Meter A and Meter B to choose the polyrhythm.
2. Pick each phrase sequencer’s length in master cycles. The app converts that into phrase steps automatically.
3. Click the step boxes to tap out rhythms. Selected steps light up and trigger when the playhead reaches them.
4. The center gear is the master wheel. It advances at one master step per tooth and defines the full cycle.
5. The wheel lanes show how each meter divides one master cycle into evenly spaced placements.
6. The phrase sequencers advance at each meter’s own pulse, so they create longer structures across repeated master cycles.
7. The Master Cycle Timeline shows one cycle. The Full Pattern Timeline shows the longer repeating phrase structure.
8. Enable audio, then adjust sounds, mute, and volume for each lane below.
