Welcome to Alan's Polyrhythm Mixer
Start with two repeating counts, tap in a simple pattern, then add extra voices to build a layered groove.
1. Choose the feel in Polyrhythm Controls. Pick Meter A, Meter B, and a tempo. Try 3 against 4 or 5 against 4 to hear the two counts weave together.
2. Tap boxes to make a rhythm. Start with the Master Cycle Pattern, then add notes to the Meter A and Meter B patterns underneath.
3. Add layers with + Voice. Each voice is another rhythm line with its own sound, so you can build from a simple pulse into a fuller groove.
4. Shape the sound in the Sound Mixer. Change instruments, mute parts, adjust volume, then Save or Share the rhythm when it feels good.
