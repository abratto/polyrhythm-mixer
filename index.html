Welcome to Alan's Polyrhythm Mixer
Start with two repeating counts, tap in a simple pattern, then add extra voices to build a layered groove.
1. Choose the feel in Polyrhythm Controls. Pick Meter A, Meter B, and a tempo. Try 3 against 4 or 5 against 4 to hear the two counts weave together.
2. Tap boxes to make a rhythm. The first pulse starts selected for the Master Cycle, Meter A, and Meter B so you can hear the groove right away.
3. Add layers with + Voice. Use Nudge to slide one rhythm, or Nudge Group to move a whole pattern together; the 1 button brings it back to the start.
4. Shape the sound in the Sound Mixer. Change instruments, mute parts, adjust volume, use the Master Click as a guide, then Save or Share.
