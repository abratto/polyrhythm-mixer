Welcome to Alan's Polyrhythm Mixer
This mixer works at two rhythmic scales: wheel lanes divide one master cycle, while phrase sequencers run at each meter's own pulse and unfold across multiple master cycles. You can also layer multiple independent voices on each sequencer lane.
1. Pick Meter A and Meter B to choose the polyrhythm.
2. Set each phrase sequencer's length in master cycles. Patterns are preserved when you change lengths.
3. Click step boxes to activate them. They trigger when the playhead reaches them.
4. Click + Voice on any sequencer lane to layer an independent pattern with its own instrument, volume, and mute.
5. The center gear is the master wheel (LCM of A and B). One rotation = one full polyrhythmic cycle.
6. Wheel lanes show evenly-spaced placements within one cycle. Phrase sequencers unfold across multiple cycles.
7. The timelines below the gears show one cycle and the full repeating pattern, with markers for every voice.
8. Enable audio, then pick instruments and adjust volume/mute for each lane and voice in the mixer console.
